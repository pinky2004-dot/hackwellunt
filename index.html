HackWell 2025

UNT's Premier Health Informatics Hackathon

October 21-22, 2025 | UNT Union Building

Register by October 18!

Innovate for a Healthier Tomorrow

Hosted by the Graduate Student Council, UNT Data Science Organization, and Health Informatics Student Organization, HackWell 2025 is a two-day hackathon uniting brilliant minds to tackle real-world healthcare challenges using Generative AI and data science. Bring your creativity and problem-solving skills to design solutions that could shape the future of healthcare technology.

Who can participate?

All current UNT students (undergraduate and graduate) are welcome.

How large can teams be?

Teams must be between 3 and 5 students. We will have a team formation event if you don't have one!

How much does it cost?

Participation is completely free. Meals and snacks will be provided.
